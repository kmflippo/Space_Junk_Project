Space Junk Home

Welcome. Did you know that there's billions of tiny pieces of space junk surrounding earth?

These pieces are dangerous because they could cause damage to satellites.

The most dangerous pieces of space junk are in geostationary orbit.

This orbit is highly prized due to its optimal distance from Earth, so it is already very crowded.
